Multivendor E-commerce Website
For Selling Laptop

Abstract:

This project aims to develop a multivendor e-commerce website tailored for the Cambodian market, specifically focusing on the sale of laptops. The platform will serve as a digital marketplace where multiple vendors can list their products, and consumers can explore, compare, and purchase laptops from various brands and sellers. The website will feature a user-friendly interface with robust functionalities to enhance both vendor and customer experiences.

The project includes the following scopes to complete:

1. User Roles: Admin, Shop Owner, Buyer
2. Admin CRUD Product Category
3. Show owner CRUD product
4. Buyer could register and login
5. Shop owner need to verify by Admin
6. Product list with filter
7. Highlighted product from select shop
8. Highlighted product from select category
9. Latest products
10. User could make an order: cart and checkout

The project will leverage modern web development technologies and frameworks to ensure a scalable, efficient, and secure e-commerce solution. By addressing the specific needs of the Cambodian market, this platform aims to bridge the gap between laptop vendors and buyers, fostering a more accessible and competitive marketplace.
